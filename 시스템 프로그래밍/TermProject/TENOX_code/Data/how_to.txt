        ! How to Play TENOX !

        * You can make a box with starting point and width / height.
            - You have to designate the starting point as the leftmost, topmost point.
        * If the sum of the numbers in the box becomes zero, Every number in the box becomes zero.
        * If all numbers become zero, you win.

        1) MakeZERO : Measure the time it takes to make all the numbers zero.
        2) TimeKILL : Let's see how many times you make zero in 100 seconds.
